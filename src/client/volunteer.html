Rarden Daily Bread




Volunteer

The Rarden Daily Bread Inc. is run by volunteers. Cooking food, going to buy supplies/ingredients, cleaning, and other duties are all performed by individuals sheerly by the goodness of their hearts. We are always looking for more volunteers so we can further help the community. We accept anyone, so all you need to do is inform us and you are able to volunteer.
