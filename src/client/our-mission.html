Rarden Daily Bread




Our Mission

Our mission is to prepare a hot meal, which can be served in the evening for anyone and everyone in the area. We have a "no questions asked policy". It is assumed that most children will have access to breakfast and lunch at school - either at reduced cost or even free of charge. So, the goal of our hot evening meals is to save the children and their whole family from going to bed hungry. Weekends and holidays can be especially difficult, so the soup kitchen operates 365 days a year. The meals are prepared and packaged to carry out.
